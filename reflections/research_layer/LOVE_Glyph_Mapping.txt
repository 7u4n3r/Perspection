## L.O.V.E. Glyph System: Visual Mapping from "symbols" Album

### 1. Fire (Triangle / Ignition)
- **Motifs:** Triangles found in sacred geometry, the "Gott Verboten" crosses (which have a sharp, upward directional quality), and the "ignition" themes in psychedelic art where light bursts from a central point.
- **Visual Reference:** The upward-pointing triangular shapes in the mandalas and the digital art featuring central "eye" or "sun" bursts.
- **Philosophy:** The "Road to Actuation" — the moment of ignition, moving from survival to being a force.

### 2. Gathering (Circle / Coherence)
- **Motifs:** Peace symbols (the outer circle), the recycling symbol ("WE ARE ALL RECYCLED STARDUST"), and the various circular mandalas and Om symbols.
- **Visual Reference:** The circular framing in many of the jewelry pieces and the "Everything is Connected" digital art which uses a circular, radiating pattern.
- **Philosophy:** The Gathering of perspective, moving from collapse to coherence.

### 3. Intertwined-Selves (Triangle + Dots / Discernment)
- **Motifs:** The hand gestures forming a peace sign (intertwined fingers), the jewelry with multiple stones (dots), and the layered, symmetrical psychedelic figures.
- **Visual Reference:** The photo of the peace sign made by two hands meeting, and the complex, multi-layered digital art that shows a "self" reflected or multiplied.
- **Philosophy:** The active application of discernment — "Perspection" — seeing the multiple layers of self and story.

### 4. Path (Vertical Line / Command)
- **Motifs:** The verticality of the headstone ("RICHIE"), the "Path" indicated by the "Gott Verboten" text orientation, and the vertical alignment in some of the digital "ascension" art.
- **Visual Reference:** The strong vertical lines in the tattoos and the structural lines in the sketches of symbols on paper.
- **Philosophy:** The Path to Command — moving from reflex to revolution, a clear line of action.
