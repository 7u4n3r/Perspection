# Perspection Services

## Service definition

Perspection is active lens provision for discernment development.

A Perspection session is not focused on motivation, diagnosis, optimization, or information transfer.

The purpose is to help someone recover clearer observation within situations where pressure, institutional framing, inherited assumptions, role expectations, or identity capture have narrowed visibility.

The goal is not certainty.
The goal is discernment.

---

# Perspection Session Structure

## Step 1: Identify the existing frame

The person describes the situation, conflict, confusion, or stuck point.

The focus is not immediately on solving the problem.
The first task is identifying the lens already shaping interpretation.

---

## Step 2: Notice the operating pattern

Perspection identifies:

- pressure dynamics
- institutional assumptions
- role conditioning
- extraction patterns
- identity loops
- framing distortions
- inherited narratives
- collapsed distinctions

The purpose is not accusation.
The purpose is increased visibility.

---

## Step 3: Provide Perspection

A different interpretive lens is introduced.

This allows the person to see the situation with expanded visibility rather than automatic reaction.

The central question becomes:

"What changes when the frame changes?"

---

## Step 4: Discernment development

The person develops the ability to distinguish:

- pressure from reality
- institutional incentives from personal values
- identity from role
- visibility from certainty
- guidance from dependency
- support from extraction

---

## Step 5: Harm reduction and navigation

Perspection does not assume immediate escape from systems.

The focus becomes:

- reducing unnecessary harm
- increasing navigational capacity
- preserving observation under pressure
- creating more conscious choice
- stabilizing discernment over time

---

# Service Types

## Perspection Session

1:1 interpretive support and discernment development.

## Perspection Circle

Group-based discernment practice through shared observation and pattern recognition.

## Perspection Immersion

Extended multi-domain discernment and systems navigation work.

## Organizational Perspection

Structural observation for organizations, teams, advocacy groups, or systems willing to examine their own operational patterns.

---

# Constitutional Boundaries

Perspection is not:

- absolute truth claims
- ideological conversion
- dependency creation
- anti-institution by default
- paranoia reinforcement
- personality worship
- coercive certainty

Perspection prioritizes:

- observational humility
- context awareness
- discernment over certainty
- visibility over ideology
- navigation over collapse
- clarity without dependency
